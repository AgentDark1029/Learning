What is a good title

The title should describe the content and the meaning of the page. The page title is very important for search engine optimization (SEO). The text is used by search engine algorithms to decide the order when listing pages in search results. The element: > defines a title in the browser toolbar<br> > provides a title for the page when it is added to favorites<br> > displays a title for the page in search engine-results<br> So, try to make the title as accurate and meaningful as possible! </p> <hr> <h2>Tag used:</h2> <p><title></p> <hr> </body> </html>
